================================================================================
TIMING EXPLAINED
================================================================================

Why 5 seconds wait after Activate?

From your screenshot, the validation process:
1. Click Activate → Loading starts
2. ~2-3 seconds → Backend validates
3. Dialog appears with result
4. Screenshot captures the result
5. Close button dismisses dialog

Total: ~5 seconds is safe to capture result

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Close button not being tapped?
→ Check coordinates: X=474, Y=608
→ Adjust ±10 pixels if needed

Screenshot shows loading instead of result?
→ Increase wait time to 6-7 seconds
→ Edit macro action #10

Validation takes longer than 5 seconds?
→ Slow internet connection
→ Increase wait to 8-10 seconds

Macro taps Close before result shows?
→ Increase wait after Activate
→ Try 6-7 seconds instead of 5
